# Aftercare Instructions

## Tooth Extraction

- Bite gently on the gauze for at least 30 minutes.
- Avoid smoking for 48 hours.
- Eat soft foods.
- Avoid drinking through a straw.
- Take prescribed medications.

---

## Teeth Whitening

- Avoid coffee, tea, and colored drinks for 48 hours.
- Avoid smoking.
- Brush your teeth regularly.

---

## Dental Implant

- Eat soft foods during the first few days.
- Maintain excellent oral hygiene.
- Attend all follow-up appointments.

---

## Root Canal Treatment

- Avoid chewing on the treated tooth until the final restoration is completed.
- Contact the clinic if severe pain or swelling occurs.

---

## Braces

- Avoid sticky and hard foods.
- Brush after every meal.
- Attend adjustment appointments every 4 to 6 weeks.
