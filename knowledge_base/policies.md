# Clinic Policies

## Appointment Policy

Appointments should be booked in advance whenever possible.

Walk-in patients are accepted based on doctor availability.

## Cancellation Policy

Appointments may be cancelled or rescheduled up to 24 hours before the scheduled time.

## Late Arrival

Patients arriving more than 15 minutes late may need to reschedule.

## Payment Methods

We accept:

- Cash
- Visa
- MasterCard
- Debit Cards
- Bank Transfer

## Refund Policy

Consultation fees are non-refundable.

Treatment refunds are reviewed on a case-by-case basis.

## Privacy Policy

Patient information and medical records are kept confidential.

Only authorized staff members can access patient records.
