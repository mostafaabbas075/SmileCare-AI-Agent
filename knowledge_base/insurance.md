# Insurance Information

## Accepted Insurance Providers

SmileCare Dental Clinic currently accepts the following insurance providers:

- AXA Egypt
- MetLife
- MedNet
- GlobeMed
- NextCare
- Allianz
- Bupa Arabia

## Insurance Verification

Patients are required to provide a valid insurance card before their appointment.

Insurance coverage is verified before treatment begins.

## Covered Services

Depending on the patient's insurance plan, the following services may be covered:

- Dental Consultation
- Tooth Extraction
- Root Canal Treatment
- Dental Fillings
- Dental X-rays

## Non-Covered Services

Most cosmetic procedures are not covered, including:

- Teeth Whitening
- Veneers
- Hollywood Smile

## Notes

Coverage depends on the patient's insurance policy.

Please contact the clinic before your appointment for insurance verification.
